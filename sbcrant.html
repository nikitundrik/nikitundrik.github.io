SANDBOXES SUCK

At the time of writing this, I have already left this shitty community and encourage you to do the same.

The definition of a sandbox for this post is: a game that tries to become an alternative to Roblox, but fails hilariously.

So, I'd like to tell my story: In 2017, I was a Roblox obsessed kid, who, just like many other Roblox obsessed kids, complained about the current state of the game and wanted to play old Roblox. Then, I heard the news that a Roblox killer exists! Its name was Blox City. But, I heard the news a bit too late, because it was already "shutdown". Then, OMG! Blox City is getting rebranded into Brick Planet! I was hyped for this game. When the site released, I made an account and waited for the client to release with huge anticipation. Then the client released! And the game was shutdown again in like 2019. Then I didn't care about it for 2 years (during this era, BP came back and then was shutdown multiple times), until I learned that it came back then I got bored and decided to check it out. I decided to create a combat guild there called Warriors of Aintia and I got allies, friends and became a "semi-celebrity" on BP. Then, this BP (omg no one could have predicted this) was shutdown again. Some BP devs then made a new platform called Buildaverse. I won't talk about it there, so I will time skip to July, 2022 when Brickverse (later known as Metawave) hired me as a client dev. The project was pretty good and the owner was great as well and... I gained fame as a client dev (or a dev in general) and was hired to work in many shitty projects. Then, I left this shithole and wrote this post.

Judging by the text alone, you can see one of the sins of this community: constant shutdowns. After a dev makes enough money from the project, he then shutdowns it and after awhile reopens it to make more money. That is called an exit scam.
